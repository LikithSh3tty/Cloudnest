# CloudNest — General FAQ

## Is there a mobile app?
Yes, CloudNest is available on iOS and Android. Mobile apps support upload, download, and offline access to marked files.

## Can I share files with people who don't have CloudNest?
Yes, via public share links. You can set an expiration date and optional password on any share link.

## What happens to my files if I stop paying?
See the Pricing & Billing doc for details on failed payments and downgrades.

## Does CloudNest have a referral program?
Yes. Referring a friend who upgrades to Pro grants both accounts 5 GB of bonus storage, up to a max of 50 GB bonus storage.

## Is there a desktop notification for sync errors?
Yes, the desktop app shows a system notification and a red icon in the system tray when a file fails to sync.

## Who do I contact for enterprise/custom plans above Team tier?
Contact sales@cloudnest-example.com. Custom enterprise agreements are handled outside the standard plan structure and are not detailed in this documentation set.
