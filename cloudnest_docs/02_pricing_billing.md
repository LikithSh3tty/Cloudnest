# CloudNest — Pricing & Billing

## Plans and Pricing
| Plan | Price | Storage | Devices |
|------|-------|---------|---------|
| Free | $0/mo | 5 GB | 2 devices |
| Pro | $9.99/mo or $99/yr | 500 GB | 5 devices |
| Team | $24.99/user/mo | 2 TB shared pool + 200 GB/user | Unlimited |

## Billing Cycle
- Monthly plans are billed on the signup date each month.
- Annual plans are billed once per year and include a ~17% discount versus monthly.
- Team plans are billed monthly per active seat; seats are prorated when added mid-cycle.

## Payment Methods
CloudNest accepts major credit/debit cards and PayPal. Bank transfer is available for Team plans with 10+ seats (contact sales).

## Upgrading and Downgrading
- Upgrades take effect immediately; you're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the cycle.
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Data exceeding the new plan's storage limit becomes read-only until you delete files or upgrade again.

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails, CloudNest retries 3 times over 7 days. After the third failed attempt, the account is downgraded to Free and files above the 5 GB limit become read-only (not deleted).

## Cancellation
You can cancel anytime from Account Settings > Billing. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the paid period. There is no partial-period refund for monthly plans.
