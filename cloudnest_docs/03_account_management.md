# CloudNest — Account Management

## Creating an Account
Sign up with email + password or via Google/Apple SSO. Email verification is required before uploading files.

## Changing Your Email
Go to Account Settings > Profile > Email. A confirmation link is sent to the new address; the change only applies after confirmation.

## Password Reset
Use "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Reset links expire after 1 hour. If you signed up via SSO, there is no CloudNest password to reset — reset it with your identity provider instead.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
2FA can be enabled under Account Settings > Security. CloudNest supports authenticator apps (TOTP) and SMS backup codes. 2FA is mandatory for all Team plan owners.

## Deleting an Account
Account deletion is permanent after a 30-day grace period. During the grace period, the account is deactivated but recoverable by logging back in. After 30 days, all files and version history are permanently erased.

## Transferring Ownership (Team Plan)
Team owners can transfer ownership to another admin under Team Settings > Members > Transfer Ownership. The outgoing owner is automatically demoted to Admin.
